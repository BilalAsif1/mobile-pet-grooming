Frequently Asked Questions

Most dogs benefit from professional grooming every 4-6 weeks. However, this can vary based on breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Long-haired breeds may need grooming every 3-4 weeks, while short-haired breeds can go 6-8 weeks.

We require proof of current rabies vaccination and recommend DHPP (distemper, hepatitis, parvovirus, parainfluenza) for dogs. For cats, we require rabies and FVRCP vaccinations. Please bring vaccination records on your first visit.

Basic wash and dry typically takes 1-2 hours, while full grooming can take 2-4 hours depending on your pet's size, coat condition, and the services requested. We'll provide an estimated completion time when you drop off your pet.
