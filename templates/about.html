About Us

Passionate about pets, dedicated to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Brad's Pet Grooming began with a heartfelt mission: to offer exceptional grooming services where pets feel safe, loved, and truly cared for. Brad believed that grooming should never be a stressful chore—for pets or their owners—but a calm, enjoyable experience centered around compassion and quality.

What started as a modest mobile setup in local neighborhoods quickly earned a reputation for professionalism, kindness, and amazing transformations. Today, Brad's Pet Grooming is one of the most trusted names in pet care, serving families across the region with a loyal following of happy pets and even happier pet parents.

At Brad’s, we don’t just clean and clip—we connect. Every session is personalized to your pet’s unique personality, needs, and comfort. Whether it’s a gentle bath, a stylish trim, or a full spa treatment, your furry friend is in expert hands that treat them like family.
